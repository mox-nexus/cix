Skill Formation & Learning

  • ◐ Shen, J.H. & Tamkin, A. (2026). [How AI Impacts Skill Formation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.20245). Anthropic. Cohen's d = 0.738 (17pp skill gap). → [skill-formation](../explanation/skill-formation), [evidence](../explanation/the-evidence)
  • ● Bastani, H. et al. (2025). [Generative AI Can Harm Learning](https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.2422633122). PNAS. -17% without guardrails, no harm with guardrails. → [problem](../explanation/the-problem), [evidence](../explanation/the-evidence)
  • ◐ Zhou, Y. et al. (2025). [Creative Scar Without Generative AI](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0160791X25002775). Technology in Society. Longitudinal: creativity drops on withdrawal, homogeneity persists months later. → [evidence](../explanation/the-evidence)

Cognitive Effects

  • ● Lee, H.P. et al. (2025). [The Impact of Generative AI on Critical Thinking](https://dl.acm.org/doi/full/10.1145/3706598.3713778). CHI 2025. β = -0.69 (AI confidence → less CT). → [problem](../explanation/the-problem), [evidence](../explanation/the-evidence)
  • ◐ Kosmyna, N. et al. (2025). [Your Brain on ChatGPT](https://www.media.mit.edu/publications/your-brain-on-chatgpt/). MIT Media Lab. 83% recall failure, reduced brain connectivity. → [problem](../explanation/the-problem), [evidence](../explanation/the-evidence)
  • ◐ Gerlich, M. (2025). [AI Tools in Society](https://www.mdpi.com/2075-4698/15/1/6). MDPI Societies. r = -0.68 (AI ↔ CT), r = -0.75 (offloading ↔ CT). → [evidence](../explanation/the-evidence)
