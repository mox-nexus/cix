Extensions that embody collaborative intelligence principles.

---

## What Belongs Here

Extensions that:

- Amplify human capability without replacing judgment
- Show reasoning at every step
- Give users control over process and outcome
- Build capability through use

## What Doesn't

Extensions that:

- Replace thinking with output consumption
- Hide reasoning behind confident answers
- Optimize for speed over learning
- Create dependency rather than capability
