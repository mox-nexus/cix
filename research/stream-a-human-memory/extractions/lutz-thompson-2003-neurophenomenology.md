## Extraction Notes

- c3/c4 split: "embodied" and "large-scale dynamical" are analytically separable theoretical commitments. One is a philosophical stance on mind-body integration; the other is a systems-neuroscience methodology.
- c6/c7/c8 correspond to the three numbered methodological challenges (i/ii/iii) in the abstract. Each is independently falsifiable and bears on different downstream concerns. c7 is especially significant for RQ4 — it names the reactivity problem: the report-generation process contaminates the phenomenon under study.
- c7 is the phenomenological vocabulary for the same problem addressed by introspection research in experimental psychology: the act of introspecting alters the mental state being reported. This directly bears on whether memex should trust user "insight" labels.
- Tier 1 for all claims despite programmatic (not directly empirical) nature — the source is peer-reviewed, authored by canonical figures (Thompson: philosopher of mind; Lutz: cognitive neuroscientist), and constitutes the foundational statement of a named research programme.
