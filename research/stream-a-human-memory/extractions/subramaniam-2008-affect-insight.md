# Extraction: Subramaniam et al 2008 — Positive Affect and Insight

## Atomic Claims

### subramaniam2008:c1
- **Claim:** Participants higher in positive mood solved more problems overall, and specifically more with insight, compared to those lower in positive mood.
- **Quote:** "Participants higher in positive mood solved more problems, and specifically more with insight, compared with participants lower in positive mood."
- **Tier:** 1 | **Confidence:** High | **RQ:** RQ1

### subramaniam2008:c2
- **Claim:** Positive mood modulated preparatory brain activity BEFORE each problem was presented, biasing subsequent solving toward insight vs analytic strategy.
- **Quote:** "Positive mood... was associated with changes in brain activity during a preparatory interval preceding each solved problem; modulation of preparatory activity in several areas biased people to solve either with insight or analytically."
- **Tier:** 1 | **Confidence:** High | **RQ:** RQ1
- **Note:** Conditions that PRODUCE insight include positive mood, and the effect is PREPARATORY not reactive. Relevant to Yash's question about what conditions produce the click.

### subramaniam2008:c3
- **Claim:** The anterior cingulate cortex (ACC) showed sensitivity to both mood and insight, with positive mood altering preparatory ACC activity to bias toward insight solving.
- **Quote:** "the ACC showed sensitivity to both mood and insight, demonstrating that positive mood alters preparatory activity in ACC, biasing participants to engage in processing conducive to insight solving."
- **Tier:** 1 | **Confidence:** High | **RQ:** RQ1

### subramaniam2008:c4
- **Claim:** Positive mood may enhance insight by modulating attention and cognitive control mechanisms via ACC, enhancing sensitivity to detect non-prepotent solution candidates.
- **Quote:** "positive mood enhances insight, at least in part, by modulating attention and cognitive control mechanisms via ACC, perhaps enhancing sensitivity to detect non-prepotent solution candidates."
- **Tier:** 1 | **Confidence:** Medium | **RQ:** RQ1
